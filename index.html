Why You Will Love Neon Trees

Bio

Formed initially by childhood friends Tyler Glenn and Chris Allen who both grew up in Murrieta, California, the band made its first home in Provo, Utah after Glenn and Allen moved there from Southern California. Eventually, the current group was rounded out by fellow Mormons Branden Campbell and Elaine Bradley. David Charles currently plays with the band as touring guitarist.

The band first started playing under the name Neon Trees in late 2005 with five original members: Tyler Glenn and Chris Allen from California, and three veterans of the Utah music scene, bass player Mike Liechty, drummer Jason Gibbons and keyboardist Nathan Evans. The band's name was inspired by the lighted trees on the In-N-Out Burger signs. They released an EP, Becoming Different People, in 2006 during their first tour through California. The EP featured promising songs like "Phones", "Sister Stereo", "Up Against the Glass". Soon after, the lineup narrowed down to four members with Branden Campbell and Elaine Bradley joining the group on bass and drums, respectively. In 2007, The Killers' drummer, Ronnie Vannucci Jr., went to watch the band play at a small club in Las Vegas as he had previously been in a band called "Attaboy Skip" with Neon Trees bassist Branden Campbell prior to joining The Killers. Ronnie was impressed, and as a result they were chosen to open for The Killers for a string of dates on their 2008 North American tour. Vannucci later helped the band sign to major label Mercury Records.

Although they had several prior independent releases, the band released its debut album on Mercury/Island Def Jam, Habits, on March 16, 2010. "Animal" was chosen as the lead single, and the band went on to preform the song live in many famous venues. It was even featured in a commercial for Las Vegas. The band's next two albums Picture Show and Pop Psychology were released in 2011 and 2014, respectively. Since that first album release, Neon Trees has enjoyed ever growing sucess. They have preformed live on talk shows, have had songs featured in commercials, and performed with famous musicians from Maroon 5 and Taylor Swift to Duran Duran and the Flaming Lips. Neon Trees has continued to rise to the top in a steady arc fueled by their catchy, dancable, pop-rock sound.
